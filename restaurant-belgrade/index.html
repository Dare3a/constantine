Hotel Constantine the Great Restaurant


07:00-22:00


The restaurant at Hotel Constantine the Great offers an inviting and refined setting, ideal for business meetings and for guests who appreciate peace and privacy.

Its thoughtfully curated menu blends international and local flavors, prepared with fresh ingredients and a touch of culinary creativity. Guests may enjoy à la carte dishes, as well as a buffet selection served for breakfast.

With a capacity of up to 70 guests, the restaurant provides a fitting venue for intimate celebrations, corporate gatherings, and small weddings.

Guests benefit from private parking and a dedicated smoking area for additional comfort.

Here, luxury is defined by atmosphere — expressed through genuine hospitality, attention to detail, and the warm welcome offered to every guest.
