If you are looking for a venue for business meetings or smaller private and corporate gatherings, the conference room at Hotel Constantine the Great is the perfect choice. Located in the very heart of the city, the hall is equipped with state-of-the-art hi-tech technology, features natural daylight and free Wi-Fi access, and can accommodate up to 80 participants. On-site parking is also available for an additional fee.

We offer carefully designed packages, a wide selection of menus and coffee break options, while our experienced and professional staff will support you in organizing an event that will leave a lasting impression on all your guests.
