Bussines meeting

A perfect place for your meeting

Belgrade offers a wide selection of restaurants and bars that can be a place for a business meeting

Besides the wide range of options, it is not always easy to make a great choice considering location, quality of service, various food and beverage selection, as well as pleasant and peaceful atmosphere. Our restaurant "Marzo" is a perfect place for your business meeting as it meets all mentioned criteria. All guests of the restaurant are entitled to use parking that is located within the hotel.

In case you need to organize a meeting for a large number of people with complete audio-visual equipment, our conference room is also at your disposal.
